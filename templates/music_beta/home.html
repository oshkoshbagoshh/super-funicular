About Us

Learn more about TFN Media

Company Overview

TFN Media is a full-service media production company specializing in music production, video production, and photography. We are dedicated to delivering high-quality media solutions for businesses and individuals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower creators and businesses with cutting-edge media solutions that captivate audiences and drive engagement. We believe in the power of storytelling through various media formats and strive to help our clients tell their stories in the most compelling way possible.

Company History

Founded in 2015, TFN Media began as a small music production studio in Nashville. Over the years, we've expanded our services to include video production, photography, and digital marketing. Today, we serve clients across the United States and have established ourselves as a leader in the media production industry.

About Nathan

Nathan is the founder and creative director of TFN Media. With over 10 years of experience in the industry, he has worked with numerous clients to bring their visions to life through various media formats.

His passion for media production and commitment to excellence has established TFN Media as a trusted name in the industry. Nathan holds a degree in Audio Engineering from Belmont University and has worked with Grammy-winning artists throughout his career.
